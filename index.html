Welcome to LibreNode!

LibreNode hosts privacy-respecting services and useful tools. We hope you find these services helpful. If you have any issues or suggestions for new services, please don't hesitate to contact us here.

Why was LibreNode created and who runs it?

LibreNode was launched on March 17, 2025 as a passion project by Tech1k, built to give back to the community. With years of experience in computing and programming, Tech1k created this platform to provide free tools and privacy-focused services for those who need them while supporting the open-source community.

Why does the site look horrible?

I'm glad you noticed the great use of CSS! In all seriousness, this site is just a landing page for our services, and it doesn't need to look pretty. The internet has become a bloated mess of excessive CSS, JS, and useless fluff. This site is designed to be simple, fast, and lightweight, giving you exactly what you need. Nothing more, nothing less. This site is written purely in HTML & CSS and the code is available here.
